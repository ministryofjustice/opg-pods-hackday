What is a GOV.UK wallet?

A GOV.UK wallet lets you keep your important government documents in one place, no matter where you go.

You can securely access and share important documents like your driving licence, lasting power of attorney (LPA), and national insurance card.

If any of your documents change, they can be updated in your wallet.

You can access your wallet online or from the GOV.UK app on your phone.
